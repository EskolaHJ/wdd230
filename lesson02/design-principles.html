Design Principles

Rule of third

The rule of third is a way to enhance the target on the view.
Which the subject takes up a third of the screen while no other object draws attention first.

Hierarchy

Hierarchy is a design principle that makes the main subject as the most catching.
Followed by the secondary subject that is less defined.
Lastly a subject that will be the last focus point.

White Space and Clean Design

White Space and Clean Design is a minimalistic way in design. Where readability is key.
Contrast makes it where you have an easier time making things out.
