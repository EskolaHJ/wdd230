What is the Rule of Hierarchy?

The Rule of Hierarchy is a fundamental principle in design that organizes elements in a way that signifies their importance, guiding the viewer’s attention through the content seamlessly. By emphasizing certain elements over others, designers can create a clear visual path that directs users’ focus effectively.

History

The concept of hierarchy in design has been utilized for centuries, evolving alongside art and architecture to enhance communication and aesthetics. It plays a crucial role in various fields, including graphic design, web design, and information architecture.

Application

By strategically sizing, coloring, and spacing elements, designers can create a clear visual path that prioritizes information effectively. This ensures that the most important information stands out and is easily noticeable to the user.
